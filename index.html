The Wedding

We’re getting married in the Burgundy Room at the Makoy Center in Hilliard. Get there at 6pm. For real. 6pm. Don’t be late, people … you know who you are ;-)

After we do the damn thing, the space will be transformed to serve heavy apps and a cash bar during the reception.

To all of our wedding guests, we want to thank you in advance for making time on a Sunday to celebrate this special moment with us. We know it’s weird to have a wedding at the end of the weekend, and many of our friends and family won’t be able to join us due to work and family commitments. If you’re one of them, we’ll party for you!

The After Party

Well, it was freaking inevitable people: there was no way our wedding day would be complete without a little Shadowbox. Around 10pm we’ll be hosting an after party at The Backstage Bistro, with a little more food and another cash bar.

This event gives for our Shadow family to share in the fun after their day of shows, and for those that couldn’t make it to the wedding. Most people are estimating that this event – like the rest of the day – will be pretty lit.
